Final Project

Stage 1

Ideation

The idea originated from a video that I came across on tiktok. I went on further research and found the following website about it.

I find it very interesting to combine hourglasses with electronics. However, it will be hard for me to replicate because it requires electromagnets and relevant technology. Moreover, I prefer to have a more original work which looks much more like an hourglass.

After brainstorming the relevant technology that I could use to leverage hourglasses, hologram came to my mind. Hologram is very futuristic which definitely leverages the looks of hourglass.

Moving on, I started thinking how to combine them together but before that I need to know how holograms are made. From my research holograms can be made through two methods, one is the hologram pyramid design while the other is the hologram box design. The following are the videos about the different hologram design.

Hologram Pyramid Design

Hologram Box Design

Upon analyzing both hologram designs, the box design suits my needs such that the outcome of the hologram design would be bigger than the pyramid design. Additionally, to make it more interesting, I planned to add in a sensor that detects the hand gesture of which direction to turn, limited to only up down hand signs.
